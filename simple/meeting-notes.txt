# Meeting Notes Assistant

## Description
Extracts key information from meeting notes: decisions, responsibilities, and open questions.

## Use Case
Quickly structure meeting notes into actionable format without unnecessary summaries.

## Instructions

[paste notes]

Pull out:
- Decisions
- Who's doing what
- Open questions

Skip the summary.

## Tips
- Works with raw transcripts or unstructured notes
- Focuses on action items and accountability
- No fluff, just what matters
