# Unresolved Action Items Tracker

## Description
Quick prompt for Microsoft 365 Copilot to surface unreplied emails and Teams conversations requiring action, with visual priority indicators.

## Use Case
For busy professionals using Microsoft 365 who need to quickly identify and prioritize pending communications from managers and important contacts.

## Instructions
